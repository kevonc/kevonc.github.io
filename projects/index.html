  • ProjectKick

    http://projectkick.herokuapp.com

    ProjectKick was born out of the desire to understand, visualize and connect with the creative culture of New York City. We collected thousands of current or successful project data points on Kickstarter.com, across hundreds of US cities going as far back as August 2009. We parsed the data with goals to discover insightful trends of the crowdfunding giant and to convey messages by visualizing data.

    ProjectKick is currently visualizing 16756 projects, 494 cities, and 50 categories.

    • Ruby on Rails
    • Javascript - D3
    • AJAX
    • PostgreSQL
    • Mechanize Gem
    • Twitter API
