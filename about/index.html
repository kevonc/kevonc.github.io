Hey, I'm Kevon Cheung!

--- In A Nutshell

  • Grew up & based in Hong Kong. Spent 7 years in Boston
  • Proud new dad with a baby girl

--- All About Venture Building

My love for creating software started when I was 12. I built a number of websites that attracted hundreds of thousands of users. Ever since then, I've been starting and advising tech projects.

I startd my career as a Software Engineer in Singapore. Then I spent 4 years at First Code Academy, a leading technology education company offering coding programs for kids aged 4 to 18 years old across Asia. When I joined First Code, we had 5 people in Hong Kong and by the time I left my role as COO, we had scaled to 4 countries with 35 full-time and 50 part-time.

Recently, I was the co-founder and CEO of Toasty, a video collaboration platform making video meetings and gatherings more interactive.

I am a generalist in venture building, and my strengths are developing new markets, driving software product growth (my current focus), building systems in marketing and customer success, and recruiting & training.
